Adobe Flash Player

What is Adobe Flash Player?

Adobe Flash Player was a software used to run multimedia content, including animations, videos, and games, on the web and on supported devices. It enabled websites to display rich, interactive content that was not possible with standard HTML. It was commonly used as a browser plugin, Adobe Flash Player is no longer supported by Adobe. As of December 2020, Adobe has stopped supporting Flash Player due to security vulnerabilities and the availability of modern web technologies like HTML5.
